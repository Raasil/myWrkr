About myWrkr

myWrkr is a job board for local volunteers that want to help their communities and make a difference.

Organizations can sign up to post volunteer listings and keep track of their volunteers.

Volunteers can make profiles and apply to positions available. A rating system ensures that organizations are getting the best volunteers
